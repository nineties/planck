Bytecode Encoding

| field length | content                        |

+--------------+--------------------------------+

+--------------+--------------------------------+
| 2 byte       | Major version                  |
+--------------+--------------------------------+
| 2 byte       | Function definition count      |
+--------------+--------------------------------+
